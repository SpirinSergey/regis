about us

St Regis Residences will give its residents the highest comfort thanks to carefully thought-out layouts designed taking into account cutting-edge trends in the construction of A-class houses.

location

490 royal residences in full decoration will be created for a chic life in the center of a modern world resort on the famous Collins Avenue. You will get to the sandy beach with a width of almost 100 meters just by going down from your apartment, and wellness sunbathing will be available daily.

The towers will be recognizable from afar thanks to the original contours and exterior. The full completion of the construction is planned for 2025.
